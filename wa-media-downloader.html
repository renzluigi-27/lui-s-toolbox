How it works
Simple as 1, 2, 3.
1

Open a chat on WhatsApp Web

Scroll up in the chat to load older media into the page before scanning.

2

Click the extension → Scan

The extension scans the visible chat and shows you what was found — docs, images, videos, and audio.

3

Select types → Download

Toggle which media types you want, then hit Download. Files are saved to your WA_Media/ folder.

Your privacy is non-negotiable.
